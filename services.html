Day-of
Coordination

"Your plans are perfect. Let us make sure they stay that way."

You've done the planning. You've chosen your vendors, confirmed your venue, and designed your dream event. Now you need someone to make sure every single piece falls into place exactly as you envisioned — without you lifting a finger on the day itself.

Our Day-of Coordination service places our experienced team on-site to run the entire show. We take over your timeline, manage your vendors, handle any surprises quietly and calmly behind the scenes, and make sure you and your family can be fully present — not running around solving problems.
