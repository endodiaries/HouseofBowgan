Commission Structure

Rewarding Every Referral

We believe great partnerships should be mutually rewarding. Every successful booking made through our referral comes with a commission fee — negotiated transparently and paid promptly. Here’s how it works across our key partner types.

📸

Creative Vendors

Commission negotiated per referral based on service value. Agreed upfront and in writing before any client introduction.

🏢

Venues & Hospitality

Venue referral commissions are structured on booking value. Terms discussed individually with each venue partner.

🤝

Corporates & Brands

Brand partnerships are tailored — from product placement to exclusive event sponsorship. Custom terms for every collaboration.

All commission arrangements are formalised in writing. We believe in clarity, fairness, and transparency in every business relationship.
