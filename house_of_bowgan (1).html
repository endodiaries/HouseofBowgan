Our Story

Where Every Moment
Becomes Legacy

House of Bowgan was born from a singular conviction — that your celebration deserves nothing short of extraordinary. What began as a premier wedding planning atelier has gracefully evolved into a full-spectrum luxury events house.

Based in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, and available to travel anywhere in the world, we bring obsessive attention to detail and an unwavering commitment to elegance to every event we touch.

From the initial vision to the final farewell, we orchestrate every element with precision, passion, and a deep understanding of what makes your occasion uniquely yours.
