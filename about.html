Who We Are

Where Every Love Story Gets Its Perfect Day

House of Bowgan was born from one simple truth: every couple deserves a wedding day that feels as extraordinary as their love story.

We are a boutique wedding planning company based in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, specialising in Nigerian, African, multicultural, and destination weddings. From the grandest details to the smallest touches, we approach every wedding with the same level of care, creativity, and commitment.

For our couples, there is no "next time." There is only this day — and it has to be perfect. Our philosophy is simple: your vision, elevated. We don't do cookie-cutter weddings. We listen, we understand, and we craft a bespoke experience that is a true reflection of who you are as a couple.

Led by founder and creative director Emem Udoh, our team of passionate planners is with you every step of the way — from the first conversation to the last dance.

Meet the Founder

The Woman Behind
House of Bowgan

"Love and weddings are my world — and I wouldn't have it any other way."

Hi, I'm Ememobong — but most people call me Emem. My obsession with weddings didn't start in a boardroom or a classroom. It started at wedding parties, where everyone else was dancing and I was quietly noticing the bride who needed someone to talk to, the guests struggling to find their seats, and the vendor running thirty minutes behind with nobody following up.

I couldn't help myself. I stepped in, solved the problem, and felt completely alive doing it. That feeling never left me.

At House of Bowgan, we believe that the details everyone else overlooks are exactly the ones that make or break a wedding. My team and I bring passion, precision, and a deep love for love itself to every event we plan. We stay current with the latest trends — but we always anchor them in the classic, elegant, timeless elements that give weddings their magic.

Being trusted with a couple's most important day is something I never take for granted. To be woven into so many love stories — each one unique, each one beautiful — is a privilege I'm grateful for every single time.
